Our Newest Projet

The Green & iQ Residential Building 14 is being developed in a privileged and quiet area in Marousi. The excellent location of the property at Irodou Attikou 14 (38°03'50.4"N 23°48'28.4"E) is located opposite a park, while it is only a 5’ walk from the Syngrou Forest , the unique lung of green and natural beauty in the Attica basin. Thus, you and your family are offered the possibility of daily recreation and enjoyment of nature. At the same time, the possibility of easy transfer is given, as the KAT metro station (Line 1) is a 5’ walk away and the KAT bus stop (Lines 500- 550 - A7 - X14) is a 3’ walk away. Additionally, next to it there is a municipal bus stop (Line 080). The nearby market of Kifissia is a 15 minute walk away, while the market of Amarousion is also a 15 minute walk away. ...more
